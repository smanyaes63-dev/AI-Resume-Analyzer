AI Resume Analyzer & Career Guidance System is a Streamlit-based application that accepts PDF resumes, extracts text, identifies technical skills, calculates ATS scores, recommends suitable job roles, performs skill-gap analysis, and provides resume improvement suggestions.

Q2. Why did you choose this project?
Answer:
Many students are unsure whether their resumes are suitable for industry requirements. I wanted to build a system that helps users evaluate their resumes and understand what skills they need for specific career paths.

Q3. How does skill extraction work?
Answer:
The resume text is converted to lowercase and compared against a predefined skill database. If a skill keyword is found, it is added to the detected skills list.

Q4. How do you calculate ATS score?
Answer:
The score is based on the presence of technical skills and important resume sections such as education, projects, experience, certifications, GitHub, and LinkedIn.

Q5. What are the limitations?
Answer:
The current version uses keyword matching, so it may miss skills written in different formats. It also does not understand context like advanced NLP systems.
